Cheeseboiger

A delicious cheeseburger that will impress your guests!

The favourite meal of white people and people of colour.

It's bread, cheese and meat put together.

Ingredients:

  • 600g Coles Australian 3 Star Beef Mince
  • 1 tbsp MasterFoods Hamburger Seasoning
  • 2 tbsp MasterFoods Squeezy Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 20g butter
  • 1 brown onion, sliced into rings
  • 6 Coles Bakery White Round Rolls, split, toasted
  • 2 tbsp MasterFoods American Mustard
  • 3 slices cheddar, halved diagonally
  • 12 slices beetroot
  • 3 dill pickles, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) MasterFoods Tomato Relish

Steps:

  1. Combine mince, MasterFoods Hamburger Seasoning and MasterFoods Squeezy Tomato Sauce in a bowl.
  2. Divide and shape the mixture into six 10cm-wide, 1.5cm-thick patties.
  3. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for 3-4 mins each side or until cooked through.
  5. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  6. Cook the onion, stirring, for 5 mins or until golden.
  7. Place roll bases on a serving platter.
  8. Place onion on bases and spoon MasterFoods American Mustard over onion.
  9. Top with the patties, cheddar, beetroot and pickles.
  10. Spread roll tops with MasterFoods Tomato Relish and place on top to serve.
